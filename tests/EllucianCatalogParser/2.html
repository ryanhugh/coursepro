MDCN 2064 - Medicine for the Disenfranchised
\r\nELECTIVE DESCRIPTION: Physical exams are provided to newly resettled refugees by care teams comprised of students, residents, and faculty physicians. For many refugees, the care is their first encounter with mainstream medicine. MS-2 coordinators manage clinic operations while MS 1-4 volunteers provide the care service and gain experience in physical exam skills and cross-cultural communication. \r\n\r\n
